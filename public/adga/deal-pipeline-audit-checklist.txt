ADGA Deal Pipeline Audit Checklist

Use this before a pipeline review or close-week standup.

1. Lead capture
- Which new leads arrived this week?
- Does every lead have source, owner, urgency, and first next action?
- Which hot leads have not been touched inside the expected response window?

2. Ownership
- Does every active deal have one accountable owner?
- Are team members, outside advisors, clients, or counterparties attached to the deal?
- Is the represented client clear?

3. Next action
- Does every active deal have a next action?
- Does every next action have a due date?
- Which actions are overdue, unclear, or waiting on another person?

4. Stage health
- Which deals have been in the same stage too long?
- Which stage confidence scores are based on evidence rather than optimism?
- Which deals need to move backward, forward, or out of the active pipeline?

5. Proof and context
- Are call notes, voice notes, transcripts, emails, files, and meeting summaries attached?
- Can another team member understand the deal without asking the owner?
- Are risks, blockers, commitments, and decisions visible?

6. Close path
- Are terms, signatures, invoice details, payment links, and handoff steps connected?
- Which deals are verbally closed but not signed or paid?
- Which invoices need to be sent, followed up, or reconciled?

7. Recovery list
- Pick the five deals most likely to stall.
- Assign one owner and one next action for each.
- Review again within 48 hours.
